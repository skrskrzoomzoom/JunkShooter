Abou Us

The Junk Shooter is an educational game created by the E-mote Company. The main purpose of this game is to educate children about healthy and unhealthy foods and give awareness on what the effects of food they eat in their health.


Junk Shooter will help the children to know about the different nutritional values of different foods. It will also help the parents to convince their children to eat healthy foods. This game has a very simple and easy to understand mechanics that is capable for children.
